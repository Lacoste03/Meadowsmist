Our Services

At Meadows mist We specialize in providing top-quality cleaning services for homes and businesses, ensuring spotless spaces and a healthier environment. Whether you need regular maintenance, deep cleaning, or move-in/move-out services, our dedicated team is here to make your space shine.

commercial Cleaning

Our commercial cleaning services deliver professional and reliable cleaning solutions tailored to businesses, offices, and commercial spaces. We ensure a spotless, hygienic, and welcoming environment, helping you maintain a clean and professional workspace. These services go beyond basic cleaning to ensure a hygienic, safe, and presentable environment for employees, clients, and customers.

What Do Our Commercial Cleaning Services Cover?

  • Office Cleaning – Regular cleaning of workspaces, including dusting, vacuuming, sanitizing desks, and emptying trash bins.
  • Deep Cleaning – Thorough cleaning of high-touch areas, carpets, upholstery, and hard-to-reach spots to maintain a germ-free environment.
  • Retail & Hospitality Cleaning – Keeping stores, restaurants, and hotels spotless for a great customer experience.

Residential Cleaning

A clean and fresh home is essential for comfort and well-being. Our residential cleaning services provide professional, reliable, and high-quality cleaning solutions to keep your home spotless. Whether you need regular maintenance, deep cleaning, or a one-time service, we ensure your living space remains clean, hygienic, and inviting.

Our Residential Cleaning Services Include:

  • Regular House Cleaning – Dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and general tidying to keep your home fresh and organized.
  • Deep Cleaning – A detailed top-to-bottom clean, tackling hard-to-reach areas, built-up grime, and stubborn dirt.
  • Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning – Ensuring homes are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized before or after moving.
  • Specialty Cleaning – Carpet, upholstery, and window cleaning to refresh and enhance your home.

post-Construction cleanup

Our post-construction cleaning services ensure your home or business is spotless, safe, and move-in ready after construction or renovation work. We handle everything from dust and debris removal to deep cleaning, so you can enjoy your newly transformed space without the hassle.

what’s Included in Our Post-Construction Cleaning?

  • Dust & Debris Removal – Eliminating dust, dirt, and leftover materials from all surfaces.
  • Floor Care – Sweeping, mopping, vacuuming, and polishing to restore floors.
  • Window & Glass Cleaning – Removing smudges, dust, and paint splatters from windows and glass surfaces.
  • Kitchen & Bathroom Detailing – Thorough cleaning and sanitization of sinks, countertops, cabinets, and appliances.
